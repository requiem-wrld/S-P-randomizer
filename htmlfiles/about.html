What is this even about?

Fortunate or Not! is a neat tool created for the purpose of obtaining relevant data about a "Random" Fortune 500 stocks.

When the user "roll the dice", they will be met with a random Ticker, and information regarding its: Symbol, Opening Price, Current Price, and the Current Change percentage (whether it is in the "red" or "green")!

At the same time, a few things will also be happening on the site. On the left hand side, users are able to view the most recent relevant news related to the ticker, which may indicates why the ticker is performing the way it is at the current time.

Furthermore, a fun little image will pop up below the ticker's information, indicating a sad "pepe the frog" gif, or a happy "pepe the frog" gif, all depending on the ticker is performing.

The fun thing about Fortunate or Not! is the surprise aspect of it, you will never know what you'll be getting!

Will you be celebrating with Wagyu steak dinner with the ticker's performance? Or will you be crying from the loss on the investment?
